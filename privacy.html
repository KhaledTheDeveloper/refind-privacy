Privacy Policy for ReFind

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

ReFind ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by the ReFind Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

User Content: We process the URLs you explicitly save using the extension to provide AI-generated summaries and categorization. This data is processed securely and stored locally on your device.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of links saved, quota usage) to improve the service. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the extension (summarizing and organizing links).
  • To manage your monthly free quota.
  • To improve the performance and stability of the extension.

3. Data Storage

Your saved links, summaries, and categories are stored locally on your device using Chrome's Local Storage API. We do not maintain a central database of your saved content.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google's Gemini API to generate summaries. When you save a link, the content is sent to this service solely for the purpose of generating the summary. Data sent to the AI model is not used to train their models.
